Trial Division - s. B.i.1(d) - plagiarism – graduate student – copying work from unattributed sources in a series of essays –  copying work from others in an application for a scholarship from a third party – marital problems and health issues insufficient extenuating circumstances – English language proficiency not an excuse for plagiarism –  no prior misconduct –  final grade of zero in the affected courses, immediate suspension for a period of five years pending expulsion, recommendation of expulsion, permanent transcript notation, and report to the provost.
