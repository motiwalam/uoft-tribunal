Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of Code – unauthorized assistance in a term test – Agreed Statement of Facts (“ASF”) –  guilty plea – Student was a Chegg.com subscriber – student requested, obtained, and used Chegg.com’s answers to the term’s questions for use on the test – finding of guilt – joint submission on penalty (“JSP”) – JSP was reasonable – a grade of zero in the course; a suspension of two years and four months; a notation on the Student’s transcript until graduation; and report to Provost with the Student’s name withheld.
