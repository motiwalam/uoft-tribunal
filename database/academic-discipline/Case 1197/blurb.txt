Trial Division – s. B.i.3(a) of Code – forged and falsified academic record – Student knowingly forged or falsified an academic record, uttered, circulated or made use of such forged, altered or falsified record, namely a degree certificate – Agreed Statement of Facts (“ASF”) – Joint Submission on Penalty (“JSP”) – guilty plea – Student admitted that he altered and falsified a degree certificate that belonged to another person and submitted it with an application for employment – finding of guilt – the JSP strikes a balance between the mitigating and aggravating factors – a five-year suspension; a notation on the transcript until graduation; and a report to the Provost for publication.
