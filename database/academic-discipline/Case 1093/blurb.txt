Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of Code – unauthorized assistance – Agreed Statement of Facts (“ASF”) – student admitted to purchasing unauthorized assistance for an assignment from an individual who provided an essay-writing service – finding of guilt – Joint Submission on Penalty (“JSP”) – student’s participation in the process, including the entering into an ASF and JSP, was a mitigating factor – the fact that this was the student’s first offence was another mitigating factor – grade of zero in the course; four-year suspension; five-year notation on student’s transcript; and report to Provost with the Student’s name withheld.
