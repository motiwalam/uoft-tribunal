Trial Division – ss. B.i.1(d) and B.i.1(f) of Code – plagiarism – purported statement of fact or a concocted source – Student admitted to plagiarizing an essay and submitting work that contained a purported statement of fact or a concocted source – Agreed Statement of Fact (“ASF”) – guilty plea accepted – Joint Submission on Penalty (“JSP”) – the admission of wrongdoing came early on – serious and deliberate nature of the offence – the detriment to the University – the JSP was reasonable – a grade of zero in the course; a three-year suspension; a four-year notation on the transcript; and a report to the Provost for publication.
