Trial Division – ss. B.i.1(a) and B.i.1(d) of Code – plagiarism – Student submitted final exam similar to three other students in the course - forgery – Student submitted a forged Verification of Student Illness Form in support of requests for exam deferrals in three courses –  Agreed Statement of Facts – guilty plea – finding of guilt  – Agreed Statement of Facts on Penalty – Student expressed remorse for their conduct – Pattern of dishonesty was an aggravating factor - Severity of the offence and detriment to University were aggravating factors – Strong need for general deterrence – JSP was reasonable  – Three-year and four-month suspension from the University; a notation on the Student’s academic record and transcript until graduation; a report to the Provost for publication.
