Trial Division – s. B.i.1(d) of the Code – unauthorized aid – using unauthorized on term tests in two different courses – hearing not attended – reasonable notice of hearing provided – finding on evidence – finding on guilt – character of the student – detriment to the University – University submission on penalty accepted – a final grade of zero in both courses; a suspension from the University for a period of three years; a notation of the sanction on the Student’s academic record for a period of four years, and a report to the Provost for publication.
