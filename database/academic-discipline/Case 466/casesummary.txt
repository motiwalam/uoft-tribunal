   Student charged under s. B.i.1(d) of the Code . The charges related to
   allegations that the Student submitted essays for two courses, each
   essay containing passages taken verbatim from internet sources. The
   Student did not attend the hearing, but submitted a Consent to the
   hearing proceeding in her absence. The parties submitted an Agreed
   Statement of Facts, and the Student pleaded guilty to the charges. The
   Panel found the Student guilty under s. B.i.1(d) . The parties also
   submitted a Joint Submission on Penalty. The Panel reviewed the joint
   submission and concluded that it reflected an appropriate penalty. The
   Panel imposed a grade assignment of zero in each of the two courses; a
   three-year suspension; a three-year notation on the Studentâs
   transcript; and a report be issued to the Provost.
