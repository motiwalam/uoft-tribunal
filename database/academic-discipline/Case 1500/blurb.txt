Trial Division – section B.i.1(a) of Code – knowingly forged or in any other way altered or falsified a document – Student submitted forged, altered or falsified Verification of Student Illness or Injury form – Student did not attend the hearing – Panel was satisfied the hearing could proceed in the Student’s absence – Agreed Statement of Facts – guilty plea – finding of guilt – Agreed Statement of Facts on Penalty – Student had one prior academic offence – Joint Submission on Penalty (JSP) – JSP was reasonable – final grade of zero in the course; three-year suspension; four-year notation on the Student’s academic record; report to the Provost for publication.
