Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of Code – unauthorized aid or assistance  – Student knowingly obtaining unauthorized assistance in a quiz – Student did not attend the hearing – Rule 17 of the University Tribunal’s Rules of Practice and Procedure  – ss. 6 and 7 of the Statutory Powers Procedure Act  – Panel was satisfied the hearing could proceed in the Student’s absence – a group of fifteen students had an answer to a particular question which all had similar characteristics  – concepts identified in the answer were not taught in the course – finding of guilt – prior offence of unauthorized assistance – there was no evidence before the Tribunal of the Student’s character, or any extenuating circumstances surround the commission of the offence – serious offence – there is a need to send a clear message to the University community – a grade of zero in the course; three-year suspension; a four-year notation on the transcript; and a report to the Provost for publication.
