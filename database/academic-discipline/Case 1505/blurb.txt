Trial Division – s. B.I.1(b) of Code – Unauthorized assistance – Unauthorized aid – Personation using electronic device – Student admitted to using concealed earpiece and camera to receive assistance in real-time from a paid tutor on two term tests – Agreed Statement of Facts – guilty plea accepted – Joint Submission on Penalty (“JSP”) – no prior offences – early admission of wrongdoing – serious and deliberate nature of the offence – detriment to the University -  JSP was reasonable – grade of zero in the course; five-year suspension; six-year notation on the Student’s transcript; and a report to the Provost for publication.
