Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of Code  – unauthorized aid – Student knowingly attempted to obtain unauthorized assistance in a final exam – Agreed Statement of Fact (“ASF”) – guilty plea – Joint Submission on Penalty (“JSP”) – Joint Book of Documents (“JBD”) – The offer of money in exchange for participating in an act of academic dishonesty is an aggravating factor  – Joint Submission on Penalty accepted – final grade of zero in the course; a four-year suspension; a five-year notation of the sanction on the Student’s transcript; and a report to the Provost for a publication.
