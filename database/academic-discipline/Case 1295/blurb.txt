Trial Division – s. B.i.3(a) of Code – forged document – forged University transcript and letter submitted to Canadian Border Services Agency (“CBSA”) – student did not attend hearing – finding of guilt – forgery or falsification of a transcript is among the most serious offences – forged transcripts and academic records negatively impact the entire University community – expulsion is almost always the outcome for a falsified transcript or academic record – up to five-year suspension until Governing Council makes its decision on expulsion, whichever comes first; up to five-year notation on the Student’s transcript until Governing Council makes its decision on expulsion; and a report to the Provost.
