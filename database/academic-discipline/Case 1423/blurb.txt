Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of Code – attempt to obtain unauthorized assistance for a final exam – acceptance of liability – agreed statement of facts (“ASF”) – student posted an advertisement on Freelancer.com asking for help – student sent payment and screenshots of exam question – finding of guilt – joint submission on penalty (“JSP”) – Panel raised concerns about the agreed-upon penalty – Panel gave effect to JSP despite concerns – grade of zero in the course; five-year suspension; notation on transcript until graduation; and report to Provost.
