Trial Division – ss. B.i.1(b) and B.ii.1(a) of Code – use of unauthorized aids – aiding and assisting other students to use or possess an unauthorized aid - student was present for part of the hearing –  Agreed Statement of Facts (“ASF”) – Student shared online document with other students to share answers on a final exam – Student submitted answers nearly identical to those submitted by other students on a term test and final exam - finding of guilt –  Joint Submission on Penalty  (“JSP”) – the offences undermined the process of academic evaluation at the University and required specific and general deterrence – The Student’s health condition at the time of the offence and expression of remorse were mitigating factors – The JSP was reasonable -  grade of zero in the course; three-year suspension; four-year notation on Student’s transcript; and report to Provost.
