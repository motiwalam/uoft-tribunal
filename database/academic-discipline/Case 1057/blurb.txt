Trial Division – s. B.i.1(a) of Code – falsified document – Student knowingly falsified an exam booklet by using a false name and address – Student made use of the falsified document – Agreed Statement of Facts (“ASF”) – Joint Book of Documents (“JBD”) – The Student attended the hearing and admitted guilt – finding of guilt – Student took responsibility for her actions – repetition of offence low as Student had completed requirements for graduation – grade of zero for the course – a 21 month suspension; a 33 month notation on the transcript; and a report to the Provost for publication.
