Trial Division – s. B.i.3(a) of Code – forgery of academic records – Student knowingly forged, circulated or made use of forged degree certificate and transcript - Student did not attend hearing – adequate notice of hearing provided – evidence established that Student submitted forged degree certificate and transcript as part of application to third party institution - no direct evidence that Student personally forged documents but Panel prepared to draw inference from evidence that Student knowingly did so – finding of guilt –  circulation of forged degree certificate and transcript extremely serious offence; damages University’s reputation, undermines trust of prospective employers and other academic institutions in University and its students and harms students who have earned their degrees – no mitigating factors as Student declined to attend hearing and provide evidence - immediate suspension for five years; recommendation of expulsion; and publication of notice of decision and sanctions with the Student’s name withheld.
