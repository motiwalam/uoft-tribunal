Trial Division – ss. B.i.1(d) and B.i.3(b) of Code – plagiarism – misrepresentation -  Agreed Statement of Facts – student’s research paper contained passages copied near verbatim from online sources – the Student misattributed passages in a paper and failed to cite the actual sources - finding of guilt – Joint Submission on Penalty (JSP) -  plagiarism is a serious offence that corrodes of academic integrity and requires a severe sanction for general deterrence – Student had extenuating circumstances due to pandemic isolation – JSP accepted - grade of zero in the course; four-year suspension; five-year notation on Student’s transcript; report to Provost
