Trial Division – ss. B.i.1(b) and B.i.1(d) of the Code – unauthorized assistance and plagiarism in an assignment – student did not attend the hearing – assignment highly and unusually similar to another student’s assignment – admission by another student that they had shared a screenshot of their work with others – finding of guilt – Appendix C to the Code provides the expectation for students and consistency in penalties sought – students cannot claim ignorance because the University delivered the message of its expectations and the seriousness of the offence repeatedly and clearly – grade of zero in the course; two-year suspension; notation of two years and eight months on transcript; and report to Provost.
