Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of Code – unauthorized aid – Student knowingly used or possessed an unauthorized aid, namely, index cards which contained information relevant to the course – Agreed Statement of Facts (“ASF”) – Joint Book of Documents (“JBD”) – Student not in attendance – representative confirmed the Student instructed them to proceed with the hearing in her absence – guilty plea – finding of guilt – Joint Submission on Penalty (“JSP”) - a final grade of zero in the course; a three-year suspension;  a notation on the transcript for just over 48 months; and a report to the Provost for publication.
