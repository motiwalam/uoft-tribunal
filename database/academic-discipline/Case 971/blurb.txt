Trial Division - s. B.i.3(f) – concocted sources in an essay – student did not attend hearing – notice provided through email - first offence – Tribunal can consider concurrent charges in the alternative – reliance on Professor’s expertise in determining whether sources are relevant –  suspension backdated to recognize delay not attributable to the Student - grade of zero in the course, two-year suspension, notation on transcript for three years, and report to the Provost with the Student’s name withheld.
