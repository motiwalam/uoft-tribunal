Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of Code  – unauthorized aid – Student knowingly used or possessed an unauthorized aid in a midterm exam – witnesses observed the Student using a cellphone during the midterm exam – the charge does not require that there be evidence that the Student actually utilized the device – finding of guilt – use of unauthorized aids during an exam is a threat to the integrity of the University’s processes for evaluating students, is unfair to other students, and jeopardizes the University’s reputation – final grade of zero in the course; a two-year suspension; a three-year notation of the sanction on the transcript; and report to Provost for publication.
