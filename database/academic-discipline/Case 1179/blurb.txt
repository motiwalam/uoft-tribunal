Trial Division – s. B.i.1(b) of the Code – unauthorized aid or unauthorized assistance – Student knowingly used or possessed an unauthorized aid or aids, or obtained unauthorized assistance in a test – Student did not appear – Rules 9 and 17 of the University Tribunal’s Rules of Practice and Procedure (“Rules”) – University’s Policy on Official Correspondence with Students – reasonable notice was provided – matter heard on its merits in the absence of the Student – the test contained answers that corresponded with a different version of the test than the version the Student received – finding of guilt – final grade of zero in the course; three-year suspension; four-year notation on transcript; and a report to the Provost for publication.
