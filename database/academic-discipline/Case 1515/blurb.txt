 Trial Division – ss. B.i.1(a) of Code – forgery – Student submitted three Verification of Student Illness Forms in support of requests for academic accommodations in three different courses – Agreed Statement of Facts – guilty plea – finding of guilt  – Agreed Statement of Facts on Penalty – Student expressed remorse for their conduct – Student faced mental health challenges at the time of the offence – Joint Submission on Penalty (“JSP”) - severity of the offence and detriment to University were aggravating factors – Student’s cooperation and mental health challenges were mitigating factors - strong need for general deterrence – accepting JSP would not bring administration of justice into disrepute - final grade of zero in each of the three courses; four-year suspension; five-year notation on Student’s academic record; and report to Provost.
