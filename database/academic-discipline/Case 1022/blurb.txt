Trial Division — s. B.i.1(a) of Code  — two counts — forged documents — Student knowingly submitted two petitions including forged University Verification of Student Illness or Injury Forms — Forged forms submitted in support of two requests for academic accommodation — Doctor named in the forms never saw the Student as a patient—Student did not attend hearing — reasonable notice of hearing provided — finding of guilt — final grade of zero in each course — five-year suspension — notation on the Student’s academic record and transcript for six years – report to be issued to Provost for publication of a notice of the decision and the sanctions imposed, with the name of the Student withheld.
