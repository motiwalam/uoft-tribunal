UTSC –  timeliness of Student’s appeal of decision from Subcommittee on Academic Appeals – Student appealed eight-month after prescribed 90-day deadline - award of a four-year honours bachelor of science (HBSc) degree in geography – appeal is barred on the grounds of timeliness – delay is egregious and without reasonable reason – given finding on timeliness, reasons on merits of the appeal are moot – appeal dismissed.
