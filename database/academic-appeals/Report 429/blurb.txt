UT - Rotman School of Management – request to summons witnesses to hearing – ss. 10.1 and 12 of the Statutory Powers Procedure Act – power conferred by s. 12(1) of the SPPA is discretionary – helpful or necessary testimony – testimony of proposed witnesses would be neither necessary nor helpful to Academic Appeals Committee (the “Committee”) in resolving factual issues before it – Use of the Committee’s process to identify the second reader would be an abuse of the Committee’s process – request to issue summons to proposed witnesses refused.
