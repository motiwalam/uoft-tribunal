School of Graduate Studies (SGS) – Student appeal of procedures adopted at re-reading of paper - SGS motion to summarily dismiss Student’s appeal as both frivolous or vexatious and on the ground that appeal brought out of time dismissed – no credible evidence to support Student’s allegation of improper communication between School and re-reader of Student’s paper, but should hear appeal as Student only had to show his argument was a possible interpretation of what transpired – not vexatious – preferable to give students exact copy of re-reader’s written assessment save for any matter that might disclose the identity of the re-reader – appeal dismissed.
